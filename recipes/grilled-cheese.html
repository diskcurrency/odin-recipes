Grilled Cheese Recipe

Apparently some people need instructions for this.

Ah, the classic grilled cheese sandwich. A go-to for those who just can't handle life's complexities. Made with a mere three ingredients – bread, cheese, and butter – this sandwich is perfect for the lazy and uninspired. But hey, who needs inspiration when you have a grilled cheese, am I right? Whether you're too lazy to cook a real meal or just want to relive your childhood memories, the grilled cheese is here to save the day. And with a variety of cheeses and breads to choose from, you can mix and match to your heart's content. So go ahead, indulge in your mediocrity. We won't judge.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread of your choice
  • 2 slices of cheese of your choice
  • 1 tablespoon of butter

Steps:

  1. Heat up your non-stick pan because this is about to get wild.
  2. Spread some butter on bread, because we all know you have some white bread and butter in your fridge.
  3. Slap that bread, buttered side down, onto the pan. You're basically a gourmet chef now.
  4. Put some cheese on top of the bread, because obviously that's what makes a grilled cheese a grilled cheese.
  5. Stick the other slice of bread on top, buttered side facing up, because who needs to count calories anyway?
  6. Cook until the bread is golden brown, or until you get distracted by a cat video on your phone and burn it.
  7. Flip it over and cook until the other side is golden brown and the cheese is melted, or until you accidentally set off your smoke alarm.
  8. Serve hot and pretend you're a master chef, even though you just made a grilled cheese.
